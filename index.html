Crime and Punishment Research Project


Welcome to our website! This is a site for our research project over the novel Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsky. This website contains a variety of elements, including a timeline for historical background, a general background guide, and a sources cited page. Feel free to browse the website using the navigation bar at the top of the page. Links to visit other pages on this site are given on the right.

The about page contains a basic description of the novel, the project, and the remainder of the pages. The Historical Context page explains the historical events going during the time period and society of the novel. The Plot gives a detailed analysis of the plot of the novel, not be confused with the less detailed outline on the About page. The Literary Criticism page uses basic tools of analysis to discuss the novel. Finally, the Sources cited page contains for the resources used in the creation of the content of this website, as well as links to some of them.

This is a project by Vishnu Iyer, Daniel Ho, and Brian Vu in Ms. Powell's 1st and 4th Periods English IV AP Class.
